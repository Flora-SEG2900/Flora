Specifications and Pricing

Specifications

Flora analyses your plants and tells you their overall health. Our high-quality products help guide the user throughout its Florian journey! Our application identify the plants and offers a brief description of the selected plant.

Along with these features, Team Flora recently added a media twist to the Flora application. Users are free to navigate more freely and add pictures of plants to their profiles which can be viewed and liked by other users. Users can also do the same and view plants from other people, allowing users to broaden their knowledge about nature.

Customers can provide themselves a Flora scanner which is put into the soil of the growing plant. By purchasing the Flora scanner, you can also get free access to the premium version of the Flora application.
Through both the application and the scanner, Flora lets you know the plant's name, alongside the current health of the plant. This information can range from if the plant is getting enough water to what is the optimal fertilizer for the plant or even if the plant is diseased or not. You may find more relevant information on both products in the Pricing section below.

Note that all of this information is tested! We collect our data from our ever-growing database to keep the information up to date and more accurate! With your help, we can make the world a better place!

*You may contact Team Flora at infoflora@flora.com for more information and/or concerns. We will be happy to assist!
